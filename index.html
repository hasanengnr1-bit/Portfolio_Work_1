About
Background

I'm a structural engineer with a BS in Civil Engineering, specializing in structural analysis and design for US-based residential and commercial construction.

At Safe Engineering Solutions, I work on structural analysis, design, and permit-ready drawing packages for projects across California, Texas, Florida, Maryland, Washington, Hawaii, New York, Colorado, and other states — ensuring full compliance with local and national building codes.

Alongside my firm work, I take on freelance structural engineering projects — helping homeowners, contractors, and architects across the US with structural calculations, permit drawings, and design consultations.

Services
What I Do
◆

Structural Analysis & Design

Complete structural design for timber, masonry, steel, and concrete systems — gravity, lateral, and connection design per governing codes.

△

Wind & Seismic Design

Lateral force-resisting system design for high-wind and seismic zones, including shear wall, diaphragm, and hold-down analysis per ASCE 7.

■

Foundation Design

Shallow and deep foundation design — spread footings, grade beams, mat slabs, and pier foundations sized for site-specific soil and load conditions.

⚙

Permit-Ready Drawings

Detailed structural plans, sections, and details with calculation packages prepared to meet local jurisdiction requirements for permit approval.
